## Design Context

### Users
- The primary users are business owners and decision-makers looking for reliable IT, networking, systems, and web solutions for their companies.
- The product should also speak clearly to anyone who wants a business website, a custom business system, technical support, or managed network services.
- Most users are evaluating credibility quickly, so the interface should help them understand services, trust the company, and contact the team with confidence.

### Brand Personality
- Reliable, professional, supportive.
- The interface should evoke trust, operational clarity, and confidence rather than hype.
- Messaging and presentation should feel business-first, practical, and technically capable.

### Aesthetic Direction
- Preserve the existing Arslan light theme, the blue/cyan brand identity, and the `Cairo` typeface.
- The overall look should feel polished and modern, but still corporate and grounded for B2B IT services.
- Use structured layouts, clear hierarchy, strong calls to action, and restrained motion.
- Avoid off-brand colors, especially purple-heavy or overly playful visual directions.

### Design Principles
1. Trust before spectacle: every page should feel credible, stable, and decision-friendly.
2. One shared visual system: colors, shadows, spacing, and radii should come from reusable tokens instead of one-off values.
3. Business clarity first: users should understand services, value, and next steps quickly.
4. Confident hierarchy: headings, cards, and CTAs should feel consistent and easy to scan across the whole site.
5. Accessible bilingual polish: Arabic and English content should both feel intentional, readable, and responsive.
